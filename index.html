Foundation — Maxwell's Equations (differential form)

These four equations govern all classical electromagnetic phenomena. The wave equation ∇²E = μ₀ε₀∂²E/∂t² follows directly — giving c = 1/√(μ₀ε₀) ≈ 3×10⁸ m/s. The FDTD simulators in this platform solve the curl equations numerically on a Yee grid.
